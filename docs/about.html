About

Data and resources

How's my flattening

https://howsmyflattening.ca/#/home

More detailed analytics for Ontario, maintained by a dedicated team.

Ontario status updates

https://data.ontario.ca/dataset/status-of-covid-19-cases-in-ontario

Bulk stats (e.g. total cases, total tests, total deaths). Updated daily ~10:30am.

Ontario cases

https://data.ontario.ca/en/dataset/confirmed-positive-cases-of-covid-19-in-ontario

Data for each cases (episode date, reporting PHU, age and gender). Updated ~daily.

Ontario's dashboard

https://covid-19.ontario.ca/data

Ontario government now has their own interactive dashboard, similar to this one.

Ontario reports

https://www.ontario.ca/page/2019-novel-coronavirus

Detailed reports and summaries from the province.

Canada's dashboard

https://www.canada.ca/en/public-health/services/diseases/2019-novel-coronavirus-infection.html

Dashboard, data, and general info about cases from Canadian government

Data for other countries

https://ourworldindata.org/coronavirus-source-data

Dataset compiled by Our World in Data with bulk stats for each country. Updated ~daily

Our World in Data

https://ourworldindata.org/coronavirus

Fantastic interactive visualizations and data reporting on the state of Covid-19 in the world
